Respir-OS: The open and accessible respirator

Read the docs

An affordable hospital grade respirator!

Made with affordable components

The Respir-OS device was designed with affordability and accesibility in mind, so it could be easily assembled in developing countries.

Completely open

All of the hardware and software that comprises the system is 100% open, which makes its lifecycle virtually endless thanks to contributions from the community.

Certified for medical use

The system has been tested and certified for use in hospital and other medical environments.
